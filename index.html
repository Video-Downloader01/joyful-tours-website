Frequently Asked Questions

Is there dedicated staff for female students?

Yes, absolutely! We follow a strict 1:10 staff ratio. A dedicated female coordinator from Joyful Tours always accompanies groups with girl students to ensure their comfort and safety 24/7.

What kind of food is provided during the trips?

We provide 100% pure vegetarian, unlimited buffet meals prepared in highly hygienic kitchens. Our focus is on healthy, home-style food that keeps students energetic during the journey.

How do parents get updates during the trip?

We create a private WhatsApp broadcast group for parents and school management. Our team shares live locations and trip photos every 3-4 hours so parents are always updated.
